The Problem

🔒

Security Policy

Your company requires locking your screen when you step away. Good practice.

💤

macOS Sleeps

Locking the screen triggers idle sleep. Your long-running AI agents get interrupted.

💻

The Workaround

Carry your laptop everywhere? That's not a solution. That's a problem.

The Solution

Holdor lives in your menu bar. One click to hold the door open for your agents.

1

Launch Holdor

A door icon appears in your menu bar. No dock icon, no windows, no clutter.

2

Select Your Apps

Toggle which agentic apps to protect. Holdor detects running apps automatically.

3

Lock & Walk Away

Lock your screen with confidence. Your agents keep working. Holdor holds the door.

Features

Menu Bar Native

Lives quietly in your menu bar. No dock icon, no main window. Just works.

Auto-Detection

Automatically detects when supported apps launch or quit. No manual PID hunting.

Smart Caffeination

Uses macOS caffeinate tied to each app's process. Display sleeps, CPU stays awake.

Remembers Preferences

Your app selections persist across restarts. Set it once, forget about it.

7+ Apps Built In

Claude, Cursor, Windsurf, VS Code, Zed, ChatGPT, and Warp. Add any app via Finder.

Lightweight

Native Swift. No Electron. No runtime. Minimal memory and CPU footprint.
